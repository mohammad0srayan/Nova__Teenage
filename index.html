Education

2025

First Year

In the first year, our focus will be on recruiting teenage learning assistants alongside AI, and building a platform to enhance the learning process.

2026

Second Year

In the second year, our focus will be on generating income for the teachers active on the platform — educators who have been nurtured and developed from the teenage users within the platform itself.

2027

Third year

In the third year, our goal will be to expand the platform internationally and build a global network of learners and educators for knowledge sharing and educational collaboration.

2028

fourth year

In the fourth year, our focus will be on enhancing the personalized learning system by analyzing user learning data, so that each student’s educational path is tailored to their individual needs and progress.
